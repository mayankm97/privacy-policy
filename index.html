Privacy Policy

Introduction

This app is designed to provide an educational and fun Augmented Reality (AR) experience where children can learn alphabets and test their knowledge through quizzes. Your privacy is very important to us.

The app requires access to your device’s camera only to display AR objects in the real world. No photos, videos, or camera data are saved or transmitted.

We do not use any third-party services for analytics, advertising, or tracking.

Children’s Privacy

This app is safe for children. Since we do not collect any data, there are no risks of data misuse.
